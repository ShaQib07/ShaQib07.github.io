01 — About

Building mobile experiences at scale

I'm a Senior Mobile Engineer with 7 years of experience designing, building, and scaling mobile products used by millions of people every day. My deepest expertise is native Android with Kotlin and Jetpack Compose, backed by strong cross-platform range — Kotlin Multiplatform (KMP) for sharing business logic, then Flutter and native iOS (Swift, SwiftUI) for cross-platform delivery.

I've worked on apps operating at 23M+ monthly active user scale, led architecture and CI/CD decisions across cross-functional teams, and mentor engineers through code review and technical guidance.
